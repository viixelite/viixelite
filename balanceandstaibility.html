Balance & Stability
Exercises

A complete guide to improving stability, coordination, and core strength.

For Beginners

Exercise Description
1. Single-Leg StandStand tall and lift one foot off the ground. Balance on the standing leg for 20–30 seconds. Switch legs.
2. Heel-to-Toe WalkWalk in a straight line. Place the heel of one foot directly in front of the toes of the other foot. Take 10–15 slow steps.
3. Bird DogStart on all fours. Extend your right arm forward and left leg backward. Hold for 2–3 seconds, then switch sides.
4. Glute Bridge HoldLie on your back with knees bent. Lift your hips until your body forms a straight line. Hold for 15–20 seconds.
5. Seated Balance (V-Sit Hold)Sit on the floor, lean back slightly, and lift your legs off the ground. Keep your back straight and hold.
6. Wall Sit with One Leg ExtendedSit against a wall in a squat position. Extend one leg straight while keeping the other bent. Hold for 10–20 seconds, then switch.
7. Side-Lying Leg Raise HoldLie on one side with both legs straight. Lift the top leg and hold. Switch sides.
8. Standing Knee Lift BalanceStand tall and lift one knee toward your chest. Hold for 10–15 seconds. Switch legs.
9. Tandem StanceStand with one foot directly in front of the other, heel touching toe. Hold for 20–30 seconds.
10. Half-Kneeling HoldMove into a lunge position with one knee on the floor. Keep your torso upright and hold for 20–30 seconds. Switch sides.

For Intermediate

Exercise Description
1. Single-Leg Deadlift (Bodyweight)Stand on one leg and hinge forward at the hips. Extend the other leg backward while keeping your back straight. Return to standing.
2. Lunge with RotationStep forward into a lunge. Rotate your torso toward the front leg (with or without a ball).
3. Step-Ups to Knee RaiseStep onto a bench or box with one leg. As you rise, lift the opposite knee toward your chest.
4. Plank with Shoulder TapHold a plank position. Lift one hand to tap the opposite shoulder while keeping hips steady. Alternate sides.
5. Side Plank with Hip LiftStart in a side plank on your elbow. Lower your hips slightly, then lift them back up.
6. Single-Leg Glute BridgeLie on your back, extend one leg straight up. Push through the other foot to lift your hips.
7. Balance Board / Bosu HoldStand on a balance board or Bosu ball. Hold posture steady for 20–30 seconds.
8. Squat Hold on ToesLower into a squat and rise onto your toes. Hold the position.
9. Reverse Lunge with Knee DriveStep back into a lunge. As you rise, drive your back knee forward and up.
10. Standing Yoga Tree PoseStand on one leg. Place the other foot on your inner thigh. Bring hands together in front of your chest.

For Advanced

Exercise Description
1. Single-Leg Squat (Pistol Squat – Assisted)Extend one leg forward. Squat down on the other leg. Use support if needed.
2. Bosu Ball SquatsStand on a Bosu ball with both feet. Perform squats while keeping balance.
3. Lateral Bounds (Skater Jumps)Jump sideways from one leg to the other. Land softly and balance before jumping again.
4. Single-Leg Box JumpsJump onto a box using one leg. Land softly on the same leg.
5. Turkish Get-Up (with Kettlebell)Lie on your back holding a kettlebell overhead. Slowly rise to a standing position while keeping it overhead. Return to the ground with control.
6. Overhead Walking LungeHold weights overhead. Perform walking lunges while keeping arms stable.
7. Plank on Stability BallPlace elbows or hands on a stability ball. Hold a plank without letting the ball move.
8. Single-Leg Hop to BalanceHop forward on one leg. Land softly and hold balance for 2–3 seconds.
9. Kettlebell Swing with Single-Leg Balance HoldPerform a kettlebell swing. At the top, lift one leg and balance briefly.
10. Bosu Ball Push-UpsPlace hands on the flat side of a Bosu ball. Perform push-ups while controlling the wobble.
