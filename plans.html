Frequently Asked Questions

Are these fitness plans really free?

Yes, all our fitness plans are completely free. We believe that everyone should have access to quality fitness guidance regardless of their budget.

Do I need any special equipment for these plans?

Most of our plans require minimal equipment. We have specific home workout plans that require no equipment at all. For gym-based plans, we indicate what equipment is needed.

How do I know which plan is right for me?

Each plan is clearly labeled with its target audience (beginner, intermediate, advanced) and goal (weight loss, muscle gain, etc.). You can also use our category filters to find plans matching your needs.

Can I combine multiple plans?

While our plans are designed to be complete programs, advanced users can combine elements from different plans. We recommend consulting with a fitness professional if you're unsure.

What if I have specific health conditions?

If you have any health concerns or pre-existing conditions, we strongly recommend consulting with a healthcare provider before starting any new fitness program.
